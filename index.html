About This Club

Mixed reality club is a Augmented/Virtual reality development club. It’s focused on developing AR/VR applications which impact our everyday life.

Mixed reality is a rapidly developing field which in the future would create a lot of career opportunities. The purpose of this club is to teach you the skills required to opt for this line of work and open a path for careers in mixed reality.

Accquire The Skills

Mixed reality is a very broad field and or will be used in every sectors of the economy. You can learn those skills in this club.

Hands On Experience

This club will provide you all the opportunity to Participate in inter-club events or projects to hone your skills.

Liberty Of Choice

There are no restrictions on you to join other clubs. You can join other clubs or augment your ideas by collaborating with other clubs and work on your own projects.
